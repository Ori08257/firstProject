Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I create a new post? Click on the "Create Post" button on the main page.
  • How can I edit or delete a post? Currently, you cannot delete a post, but if it's important, please contact us.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Create a New Post

  1. Get down and click the "Create Post" button on the main page.
  2. Insert your title and description.
  3. Click the "Send" button and you're done.
